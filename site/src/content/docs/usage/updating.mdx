## Method 1

<Steps>
1. Click on the Violentmonkey icon

2. Click on the gear to open your dashboard

3. Press the update icon on Gimloader
</Steps>

## Method 2

You can also just click on [this link](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TheLazySquid/GimLoader/main/build/bundle.user.js) again and hit "reinstall".
