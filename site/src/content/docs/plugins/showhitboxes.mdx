---
title: ShowHitboxes
description: A Gimloader plugin that lets you see your hitbox
---

import ButtonInstall from '../../../components/ButtonInstall.svelte';

ShowHitboxes enables Gimkit's built-in debug mode, which shows you various things: your hitbox, your velocity, and the hitboxes of static objects. This cannot be activated mid-game, you will need to reload.
