description: A Gimloader plugin that lets you see your hitbox
---

import ButtonInstall from '../../../components/ButtonInstall.svelte';

ShowHitboxes will draw your player's collider (a red capsule) when in 2d modes. It will look very choppy, since Gimkit's physics run at a much lower update rate than its graphics.
